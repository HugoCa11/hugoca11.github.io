Lingo Plaza

Lingo Plaza is an e-learning platform created to teach Spanish. This app is focused in kids between 4 and 12 years old. The app is developed using Unity and compiled on WebGL and stored on AWS. Click here to check the web app.

While I worked at Lingo Plaza my main tasks consisted on:

  • Created content for the unit 3. I created 22 lessons each one with up to 21 activities using C#, Visual Studio, Photoshop and Unity Engine.
  • Managed On-premise backend services to update WebGL Unity platform weekly
  • Managed accounts and users with testing and support proposes
