Professional Expereince

All begins on 2017

My first professional experience began during my 7th semester at university as part of the "Semestre i" (i Semester) program. Then, in 2018, I started an internship at one of the largest Coca-Cola bottlers in the world, working as a SAP Access and Profiles Intern. A year later, in 2019, just before graduating, I joined the Salesforce Academy, a decision that shaped my career for nearly three years as a Salesforce Developer, contributing to major projects for financial industry clients.
While working as a Salesforce Developer, I decided to explore a new path as an Interactive Developer, which led me to gain experience as a Unity Developer. In December 2021, I took a career break to complete a VR/AR Design and Development diploma, setting the foundation for my future in immersive technology. Now, I am applying my development expertise and creativity at Greensky Games, where I contribute to exciting VR projects, pushing the boundaries of interactive experiences.
