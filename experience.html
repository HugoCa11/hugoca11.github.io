Professional Expereince

All begins on 2017

My first professional approach was when I was studying the 7th semester of my career, during the "Semestre i" (i Semester). Then on 2018 I started an internship in one of the largest Coca-Cola bottler in the world as a SAP Acess and Profiles intern. A year later, in 2019, just before I graduated from college, I entered the Salesforce Academy, a move that would determine my path for nearly three years as a Salesforce developer.
At the same time, while I was working as Salesforce Developer I decided to start with a new career path as interactive developer, in this time is when I had the opportunity to work as Unity Developer. Finally, since December 2021, I took a career break to study a diploma on VR/AR Design and Development, which is the path that I want to take from now on.
